Built for students, managed by admins.

EduLib streamlines every aspect of library management — from cataloging new arrivals to tracking borrowed books and managing member accounts. Designed as a college miniproject with real-world architecture.

  • Students can borrow up to 3 books; faculty up to 5
  • Default 14-day lending period with ₹2/day overdue fine
  • Members with unpaid fines are restricted from new borrowings
  • Full admin control over books, members & transactions
